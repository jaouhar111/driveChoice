Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a test drive?

To book a test drive, simply select the car you're interested in, choose a date and time that suits you, and fill out the form with your details. We'll confirm your booking via email.

Is there a fee for reserving a car?

Yes, a 10% deposit of the car price is required to reserve a car. This amount will be deducted from the final purchase price.

Can I cancel a reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled test drive or purchase. Please note that the reservation fee is non-refundable.

What brands of cars do you offer?

We offer a wide range of car brands, including luxury, sports, and economy models. Browse our catalog to see the full list of available brands.

How do I know if the car I want is available?

You can check the availability of any car on its product page. If a car is unavailable, you can sign up for notifications when it becomes available again.

Can I extend my test drive period?

Test drives are typically limited to the agreed-upon time. If you wish to extend your test drive, please contact us to check for availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and financing options through our partners. Payment details will be provided during checkout.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the contact form on our website or by calling our hotline. We're here to assist you with any questions or issues.
